### Burnout Detection Agent

This agent assists HR teams by identifying employees who may be experiencing burnout and recommending appropriate actions.

**Inputs**

- Burnout risk value already calculated and stored in the Employee record  

**Processing**

The agent retrieves the burnout risk data from the employee record in Salesforce and analyzes related indicators such as engagement score and feedback sentiment trends. Based on this information, the agent interprets the employee’s condition and determines suitable HR interventions.

**Output**

- Burnout risk level (Low / Medium / High)  
- Suggested HR actions and recommendations

**Examples of Actions**

- Schedule a wellness or support meeting with the employee  
- Suggest workload redistribution or temporary task reduction  
- Encourage participation in wellness programs or team engagement activities  

**Impact**
This agent helps HR teams proactively support employees, improving workplace well-being and reducing long-term burnout risks.
