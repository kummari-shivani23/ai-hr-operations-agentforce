# Referral Agent

## *Overview*

The **Referral Agent** simplifies the employee referral process by allowing employees to submit candidate referrals easily.  
The agent captures referral details and automatically notifies HR, making the hiring process faster and more organized.

This helps companies leverage employee networks to identify potential candidates efficiently.

---

## *Inputs*

* **Referral Name**
* **Candidate Role**
* **Candidate Experience**
* **Candidate Details**

---

## *Processing*

* An employee submits a referral through the system.
* The **Referral Agent** collects candidate details such as name, role, and experience.
* The agent validates and stores the referral information in the **Referral object**.
* HR is automatically notified about the new referral.
* The referral can then be reviewed by HR for further hiring processes.

---

## *Output*

* New referral record created
* HR notification for the submitted referral
* Candidate information stored for recruitment review

---

## *Impact*

* Simplifies the employee referral process
* Ensures HR receives referrals instantly
* Helps organizations discover potential candidates faster
